### CityCamp

Save the date for World CityCamp Day on September 19, 2026! CityCamp is a community-driven unconference that brings together residents, government staff, civic leaders, technologists, designers, entrepreneurs, and journalists to collaborate on local challenges and opportunities. Since it's beginnings in Chicago in 2010, CityCamp events have empowered communities worldwide to explore topics ranging from civic engagement and open government to housing, transportation, education, and public health. [Nicole Dan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/nicolekdan/) from FCI is helping coordinate this year's efforts, and we encourage everyone interested in strengthening their community through collaboration and innovation to get involved. Please email [nicole.dan@floridainnovation.org](mailto:nicole.dan@floridainnovation.org) to receive updates on weekly CityCamp planning meetings. [Watch future ACT newsletters for updates](https://eepurl.com/ithxXU) on CityCamp Day activities and ways to participate.

### Congress

Mark your calendars for ACT Congress 2026! Hosted this year by [Florida Community Innovation (FCI)](https://floridainnovation.org/) in Orlando, this annual gathering brings together civic technologists, community advocates, nonprofit leaders, and volunteers from across the country to connect, collaborate, and build technology for social good. With unconference sessions, hands-on volunteering opportunities, and skill-building workshops led by ACT leaders. This will be a unique moment to learn, share ideas, and strengthen the civic tech community. [Nicole Dan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/nicolekdan/) and [Erin Enabnit](https://www.linkedin.com/in/erinenabnit/) from FCI are helping coordinate this year's event. Dates and registration details are still being finalized, and there will be a cost to attend. [Stay tuned to the ACT Newsletter](https://eepurl.com/ithxXU) for updates and information on how to get involved.
